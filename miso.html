Redesign of MISO Market Portal

Our team is working on a brand-new design of Market Portal website of MISO. We conducted user research, created low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, and will conduct usability testing to deliver the best design possible. At the end, we will deliver a formal presentation of our findings to the client.
